Creating and renaming an organization

Codacy Team
September 14, 2018 13:09
Follow

To create an organization, please click on your avatar and select Organizations, type the name in the Create new organization field and click on Create New.

Renaming your organization

You can always rename an organization but remember:

  • You won't be able to change it back if someone else registers the same organization name.
  • This may break all your project's URLs.
To ease the migration, URLs will be valid for a period of at least 15 days.
