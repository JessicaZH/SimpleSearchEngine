UNT's Newest Residence Hall Available to Honors College Students

Rawlins Hall is designed with the academically-focused student in mind, offering an abundance of study rooms, small meeting space, music practice rooms, and activity areas. The connection of Rawlins Hall to the Honors College helps create a supportive atmosphere for residents.

Why Honors at UNT?

Here at the Honors College, we prepare students for academic challenges and intellectual growth in a world of opportunity. We support our students by preparing them for graduate study, career building, and all the learning pursuits of a lifetime.
