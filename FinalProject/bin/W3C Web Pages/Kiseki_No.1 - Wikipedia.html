"Kiseki" (Japanese: miracle) and "No. 1" are songs on BoA's seventh single in Japan. It is also her seventh-highest-selling single. This single was featured on her second full Japanese studio album Valenti as a promotional track along with No. 1 and Flower. Video premiered in October 2002.
