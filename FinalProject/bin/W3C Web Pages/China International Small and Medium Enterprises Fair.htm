Background Information of CISMEF and Co-host Mechanism

CISMEF aims to build a platform of "display, trade, exchange and cooperation" for SMEs home and abroad so as to increase understanding, strengthen cooperation, expand exchanges and strike common development for China's SMEs and their foreign counterparts.
