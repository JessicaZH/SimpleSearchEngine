Newsham becomes first new Gladiators signing of the summer

May 25, 2016 | More News

Matlock Town have sealed the signing of twenty nine year old striker Marc Newsham formally of Gainsborough Trinty.

Marc began his career with Rotherham United making forty eight appearances for the first team and scoring seven goals during which time he had loan spells with Sheffield FC, Gainsborough Trinty and Ilkeston Town respectively.

After leaving Rotherham United in 2009, Marc went on to join Boston United where he became a club legend scoring a remarkable 110 goals in 209 games during a six year spell before departing to Gainsborough Trinty last season where he made twenty appearances and scored nine goals.
