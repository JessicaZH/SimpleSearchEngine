No. 1 is the first studio album by the South Korean boy group Teen Top. The track 사랑하고 샆어 (I wanna love) was released on the February 15th as a pre-released track. The full album was released both digitally and physically on February 25 using 긴 생머리 그녀 (Miss Right) as the promotional track.

A repackaged edition titled No.1 Repackage Special Edition was released to thank fans for their support.[1]

Background[edit]

Teen Top's leader, C.A.P, contributed in writing lyrics for the song, Mr.Bang, along with Maboos and Chakun (from Electroboyz).[2]
