Practical Experience

We work with the best so you can too. The Dallas-Fort Worth area offers unmatched career advancement options, and UNT offers unmatched access to top employers. You’ll make valuable connections through internships and elevated professional experiences, and you may even land a new job as a result.

Intern to Employee

We’re a proud partner of the Dallas Cowboys. For UNT students like Alex Shaw (’18 M.S., Kinesiology), that means new academic experiences, including an internship with Cowboys Fit that led to full-time employment.

Corporate Consultation

M.B.A. students with a focus on marketing serve as consultants for national corporations from 7-Eleven to AT&T.

Employers seek out UNT graduates because they're known for being successful leaders in the workforce
