It appears incorrectly here due to technical restrictions. 1 Studio album by Linda Király Released December 1, 2003 (Hungary)[1] Genre R&B, Soul, Pop Length 66:16 Label King Music Management Singles from #1 "Szerelem utolsó vérig" Released: 2002 "Clubsong" Released: 2002 "Holla" Released: 2004 "És mégis" Released: November 8, 2004[2] #1 is the first album by Hungarian singer Linda Király. Though it includes songs both in English and Hungarian, it was released only in Hungary, where it reached #19 on the official albums chart.[3] It includes two of her previous hits, "Szerelem utolsó vérig" (the theme song of the movie of the same title) and "Clubsong" (feat.
