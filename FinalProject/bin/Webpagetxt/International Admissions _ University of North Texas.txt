Admissions International Admissions Begin your university experience in the heart of Texas. Located in Denton, UNT is a student-centered public research university committed to preparing students to become thoughtful, engaged citizens of the world. One of the most diverse universities in the nation, UNT is home to 2,500 international students from 145 countries. It's easy to apply!
