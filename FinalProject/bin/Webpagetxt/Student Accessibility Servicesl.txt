Located in Dillon Hall on the main campus of the University of Windsor, Student Accessibility Services (SAS) is committed to working with the entire campus community in creating equal access to higher education for all academically qualified students who may have disabilities. We strive to foster an inclusive climate of mutual respect and understanding on the UWindsor campus, so that all students are welcome and able to contribute to the fabric of the university community. If you would like more information about how the University of Windsor generally supports accessibility, human rights and equity on campus, please visit the Office of Human Rights, Equity and Accessibility. SAS is further committed to the promotion of heightened awareness, on campus and in the community, about the responsibility we all share to provide equal access to higher education for all qualified students.
