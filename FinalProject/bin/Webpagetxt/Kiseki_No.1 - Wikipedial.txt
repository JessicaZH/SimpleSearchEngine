(February 2007) (Learn how and when to remove this template message) "Kiseki / No. 1" Single by BoA from the album Valenti Released September 19, 2002 Format CD single Recorded 2003 Genre J-pop electropop dance-pop Label Avex Trax Songwriter(s) Kiseki: Natsumi Watanabe, Kosuke Morimoto No.1: Sigurd Rosnes BoA singles chronology "Valenti" (2002) "Kiseki / No. 1" (2002) "String Module Error: Match not found" (2003) "Kiseki" (Japanese: miracle) and "No. 1" are songs on BoA's seventh single in Japan. It is also her seventh-highest-selling single. This single was featured on her second full Japanese studio album Valenti as a promotional track along with No. 1 and Flower. Video premiered in October 2002.
