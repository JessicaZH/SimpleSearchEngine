Producer Seth Farber Odetta chronology Absolutely the Best (2000) Looking for a Home (2001) The Tradition Masters (2002) Professional ratings Review scores Source Rating Allmusic [1] Looking for a Home is an album by American folk singer Odetta, released in 2001. It consists of songs written and/or performed by Huddie Ledbetter, better known as Leadbelly. Track listing[edit] All songs by Huddie Ledbetter, also known as Leadbelly unless otherwise noted.
