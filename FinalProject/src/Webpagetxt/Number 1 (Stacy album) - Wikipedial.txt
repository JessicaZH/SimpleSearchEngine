Altimet" Released: 4 April 2015 #1 is the third[1] studio album of Akademi Fantasia season 6 winner Stacy which was launched on 14 August 2014[2] in Malaysia. The album is produced by Astro and distributed by Warner Music Group as a double album. The album incorporates similar styles of music from her previous album, which includes pop and dancepop. Stacy chose the album title because she likes the term "Number 1" and always aiming to be a winner. She enlisted popular local music producers such as Audi Mok, Kimberly Chin, Omar K.[1] The launching of the album was held in Zouk, Kuala Lumpur, in a mini-concert concept. Contents 1 Production 1.1 Singles 2 Track listing 2.1 Disc 1 2.2 Disc 2 3 Awards and nominations 4 References Production[edit] Stacy collaboration with Indonesian boyband Smash in November 2013, was later added to the album. Stacy received widespread acclaim for this album with critics saying the songs are "fresh", "catchy" and of "high quality".[1] This album marks the first time Stacy is involved in the production of her album from the album cover to the songs and the lyrics, even directing the music video of "Gilalah".[1] Stacy stated that the album was her gift to her loyal fans, for supporting her throughout her career.[1] There are 19 tracks in the album altogether, 10 of which are original and the remaining are remixes.[2] Singles[edit] Majority of the singles in the album were commercially successful. The lead single peaked atop of Muzik Muzik Chart, which brought her to the prestigious stage of Anugerah Juara Lagu 29. The second single "Patah (Broken)" served as a promotional single. Despite showing powerful vocal performance in the track, it failed to crack the music charts. The accompanying video has since been viewed over a million times.[3] "Not for Sale" was released as the third single, marking Stacy's return to radio top 10 charts after about a year. The song peaked at No.7 on Era.fm chart, No.3 on Hot.fm Top 30 and peaked at No.1 on Muzik Muzik Chart.
