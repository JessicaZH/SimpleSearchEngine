Heteronuclear molecule - Wikipedia Heteronuclear molecule From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia Jump to navigation Jump to search Heteronuclear molecules Heteronuclear molecules, or heteronuclear species, are molecules composed of more than one type of element, for example, HCl. In heteronuclear molecules e.g. HCl where bonded atoms are of different elements, the molecules may become polar due to electronegativity differences.
