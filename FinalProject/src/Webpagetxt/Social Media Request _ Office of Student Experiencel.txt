Using Social Media can assist you with: Increasing attendance at events Communicating exciting and/or important messages Finding volunteers and much more How do I Submit a Request to Post Something? Anyone can submit a request to be featured on our social media pages. We really like sharing exciting information from other on-campus departments, student groups and even events hosted off-campus that students would love to attend. Please send an email stating your request to Jacqueline Veres. All requests will be reviewed before posting. Please allow 2-3 business days for a response to a request.
