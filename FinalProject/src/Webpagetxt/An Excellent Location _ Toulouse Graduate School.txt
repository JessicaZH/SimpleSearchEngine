You can enjoy a wide variety of events, many of them free and family-friendly. Some of our favorites include: Denton Arts & Jazz Festival Holiday Lighting Festival Community runs and bike rides Thin Line Film Festival Community yoga classes Spend a little time here and it's easy to see why Denton is popular with young professionals. • Near Dallas, one of Forbes’ Top 10 cities for job growth • The 12th healthiest housing market among small U.S. cities • Two nearby lakes and a 2,000-acre nature preserve • Fun, free and family-friendly activities for all ages • A cost of living lower than the U.S. average Degree options where you want, when you want. We call Denton home, but UNT offers you more choices in how and where you study with five locations across the North Texas region and a wide range of online options. You can take courses during the day, evening or 100% online — either full- or part-time.
