How do I get it? All employees have a Passport York account. Effective August 18, 2016: New Employee instructions for setting up Passport York and Central Email Account. If you have forgotten or need to reset your Passport York password then please follow one of the password changing methods. How can I obtain Guest / Special Event accounts? See information about obtaining Guest / Special Event accounts What are some examples of Passport York authenticated applications? Examples of Passport York authenticated applications HR Self Serve eReports Manage My Services Grades Input Class List Maximo IT Help Classroom Equipment Ordering System (CEO) Glendon Campus Classroom Equipment Ordering (GEO) Passport York-protected web pages or websites Other accounts and passwords: While Passport York provides single login and easy access to a wide range of services, including administrative systems, there are other services that have separate login methods, usernames and passwords, distinct from Passport York, e.g. Central Web, Lotus Notes email, MyMail, Faculty-supported email.
