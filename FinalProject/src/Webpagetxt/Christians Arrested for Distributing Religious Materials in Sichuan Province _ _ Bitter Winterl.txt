The four believers were distributing flyers with religious information on a university grounds where its security detained them. Later on, they were brought to a police station, charged with “obstructing government administration” and released at 3:30 a.m. the next morning. This was the fourth incident this year when the police prevented Autumn Rain Blessing Church’s members from evangelizing.
