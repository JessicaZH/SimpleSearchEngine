Press and partnerships If you're a member of the press looking to contact Wikipedia, or have a business proposal for us. Some of Wikipedia's servers; your donations help us keep them running. Wikipedia and its fellow sites are hosted by the Wikimedia Foundation, a non-profit organisation based in the United States. Sites like Google or Yahoo are hosted on thousands of servers, with thousands of employees; we have around 800 servers and around 300 staff, and cover our costs through donations—almost all from members of the public. If you're interested in donating to Wikipedia, you can do so here. If you've got questions about where your money goes, what we spend it on or what our plans are for the future, you can find out here. Should you have any problems donating, or other questions, please email donate@wikimedia.org .
