About China Hi-Tech Fair

China Hi-Tech Fair (CHTF) 2017, with the theme of "Innovation-driven Development & Supply Quality Upgrade", will be held from Nov 16 to 21 in Shenzhen. Jointly hosted by several ministries and commissions under the State Council and Shenzhen Municipal People's Government, CHTF has been successfully held for 18 years.
