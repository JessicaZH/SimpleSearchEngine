Chemistry, physics, and energy[edit]

  • Gasoline, a refined fossil fuel in non-gaseous liquid form, also called petrol
  • Any fuel gas regardless of its physical state
    • Natural gas, a gaseous fossil fuel consisting primarily of methane
    • Liquefied petroleum gas, also called "cooking gas", which includes propane and butane
    • Autogas, a type of LPG used in motor vehicles
  • Gas giant, a type of planet
  • GaS, chemical formula for Gallium(II) sulfide

Biology and medicine[edit]

  • Streptococcus pyogenes, a bacterium commonly known as Group A Strep, or GAS
  • Gas, slang for an anaesthetic
  • Gas, slang for poison gas:
    • Chemical weapon and chemical warfare
    • Gas chamber
    • Poison
    • Toxicology
  • The medical effects of gas being generated in the human gastrointestinal tract:
    • Bloating
    • Burping
    • Flatulence
  • General Adaptation Syndrome, a model describing responses to stress
  • Global Assessment Scale, later Global Assessment of Functioning, in mental health
  • Goal attainment scaling, a method for monitoring client progress in therapy
