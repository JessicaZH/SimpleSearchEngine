50+ Years of Experience Preparing Nurses

Â 

The Faculty of Nursing provides many opportunities for you to gain valuable, hands-on experience that will ensure you receive a well-rounded education founded both in academic theory and real-world application.

Our facility has completed a $4.2-million expansion, making it an exciting time within the Faculty of Nursing. View our Strategic Plan for 2012-2017.

Our accredited undergraduate programs offer the following:

  • Clinical experiences starting in year one in hospital and community settings,
  • Hands-on learning with state-of-the-art simulation experiences using high-fidelity mannequins that are "human-like"
  • Clinical skills and practice opportunities in our Nursing labs
  • Opportunity to live on campus on a floor devoted to Nursing

As a nurse, your role may be clinical practitioner, administrator, teacher or researcher.Â  You may practise in many settings, from hospital to home, in clinics or industry, to name just a few. You may also pursue graduate education.
