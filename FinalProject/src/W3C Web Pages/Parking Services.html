Parking at UWindsor

Notice of Temporary Parking Lot Closure 

Due to scheduled construction for the Lancer Sport and Recreation Centre, the St. Denis parking lot will be temporarily closed for the duration of October 15, 2018, until approximately December 1, 2018.

Access to College Ave. in front of the St. Denis Centre will be closed from October 15, 2018, until approximately October 26, 2018. We encourage students and faculty to use Huron Church to access the west portion of College Ave. 

If you are a student parking in the St. Denis lot, please review the student temporary closure page for more information regarding the relocation of parking.

If you are faculty parking in the St. Denis lot, please review the staff temporary closure page for more information regarding the relocation of parking.

The University of Windsor has over 2,800 parking spaces on campus. Access to these lots is controlled by parking permits, meters and gates during enforcement hours (Every day from 7am to 11pm).

Parking options are available to:

  • Students,
  • Faculty and Staff,
  • Visitors and Non-Faculty & Staff
  • People with accessible parking needs.

Payments are accepted by:

  • American Express
  • Visa
  • Mastercard
  • Debit Card
  • UWin Card
  • Cash

Unfortunately, we no longer accept personal cheques.

Contact Campus Parking Services

If you have any parking related questions, please contact our office. We are located at:

Campus Parking Services
Joyce Entrepreneurship Centre, 1st Floor
(519) 253-3000 Ext. 2413
parking@uwindsor.ca

Office hours: Monday - Friday 8:00am to 4:00pm
