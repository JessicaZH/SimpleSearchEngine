Social Media Request

Why Social Media? 

Using Social Media can assist you with:

  • Increasing attendance at events
  • Communicating exciting and/or important messages
  • Finding volunteers
  • and much more

How do I Submit a Request to Post Something? 

Anyone can submit a request to be featured on our social media pages. We really like sharing exciting information from other on-campus departments, student groups and even events hosted off-campus that students would love to attend.

Please send an email stating your request to Jacqueline Veres. All requests will be reviewed before posting. Please allow 2-3 business days for a response to a request. 

Direct Contact Information
Jacqueline Veres
Communications Assistant
Email: Jacqueline.Veres@uwindsor.ca
Tel: 519-253-3000 ext. 4250

What do I do After I Submitted my Request? 

Sit back and relax as students engage with your post!
