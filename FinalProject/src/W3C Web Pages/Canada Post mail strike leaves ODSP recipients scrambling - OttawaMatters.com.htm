Canada Post mail strike leaves ODSP recipients scrambling

Additional details have been released regarding the delivery of Ontario disability cheques for next month.
0
Nov 28, 2018 10:57 AM by: Victoria Williston
Photo credit/ Canada Post

Even though postal workers have been legislated back to work, the reliability of mail delivery remains uncertain in Canada. 

The government has set up a pick up a location in Ottawa, for those still waiting on their disability cheque, at the Sala San Marco Centre at 215 Preston St. 

The centre will be open Thursday, Friday and Monday from 8:30 a.m. until 5 p.m., however the cheques will not be payable until November 30. 

For additional information contact 613-234-1188.
